## Context and Orientation

`portfolio_xray.json` is generated by the Portfolio X-Ray builder in `src/portfolio_xray.py`. Before this change it already contained top-level product blocks for 2.1, 2.2, and 2.3, plus legacy sections under `sections`. The legacy hidden risk implementation is `sections.hidden_risk_detector` inside `src/portfolio_xray.py`; it is preserved. The new product-facing implementation lives in `src/block_2_4_hidden_exposure.py` and is called from `build_portfolio_xray_v2` only after `block_2_1_asset_allocation`, `block_2_2_portfolio_metrics`, and `block_2_3_factor_exposure` are built.

‚ÄúRule-based‚Äù means the block uses explicit numeric thresholds and signal weights in code. ‚ÄúHeuristic v1‚Äù means those thresholds are diagnostic product rules, not optimizer constraints, mandate gates, or canonical financial formulas. ‚ÄúEvidence‚Äù means each metric cited in an alert is a structured object with metric name, value, threshold, direction, source block, and interpretation.

## Product Contract

The new top-level key is `block_2_4_hidden_exposure`. It contains `block`, `block_id`, `block_name`, `status`, `summary`, `alerts`, `top_hidden_risks`, `data_quality_warnings`, and `diagnostics_meta`. `status` is `ok`, `partial`, or `unavailable` depending on whether all, some, or none of the six alerts have enough evidence.

The six alert ids are `hidden_equity_beta`, `duration_concentration`, `credit_liquidity_risk`, `correlation_concentration`, `weak_hedge_behavior`, and `tail_risk`. Each alert contains `status`, `score`, `evidence`, `explanation`, `why_it_matters`, `next_tests`, `confidence`, `data_quality_warnings`, `insufficient_evidence_reasons`, and `calculation_notes`. Alert status is `Low`, `Medium`, `High`, or `Unavailable`; scores map to Low 0‚Äì39, Medium 40‚Äì69, High 70‚Äì100, and `Unavailable` has `score: null.

Each evidence item must have exactly `metric`, `value`, `threshold`, `direction`, `source`, and `interpretation`. Direction is one of `above_threshold`, `below_threshold`, `present`, `missing`, or `conflicting`. Source is one of `block_2_1`, `block_2_2`, `block_2_3`, `taxonomy`, or `portfolio_analytics.
